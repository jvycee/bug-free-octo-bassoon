Hey there! I'm Jaycee Lewis, a community manager and artist based in Albuquerque, New Mexico. For the last 5 years, I've been on a mission to help creatives flourish. My passion revolves around guiding others to nurture their creative practice, proving that even dedicating 20 minutes a day can spark incredible transformations.

As a community manager, I cultivate spaces where artists connect and collaborate, such as Instagram and Threads. Being an artist myself, I deeply understand the artistic journey. I'm a firm believer in the power of small daily commitments, which is why I write blogs, newsletters, and create courses and books that inspire individuals to embrace their artistic identity.

Outside the creative realm, I find joy in cooking, hiking, and photography. These activities not only fuel my inspiration but also ground me in the beauty of the world. I also have a knack for coding simple websites, a skill that lets me share my creative endeavors effectively.

Thanks for getting to know me! I'm excited about the chance to connect and collaborate with fellow creatives. Feel free to reach out if you're interested in discussing building your creative practice, art, community building.
